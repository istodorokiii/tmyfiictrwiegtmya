Kim Dokja wasn't a part of the story to begin
with. He was meant to be a reader, never part
of the story. Yet, it became a world where he
could find companionship and family,
something he never had. A world where he
finally felt like his existence had a purpose.
He finally had a reason to live for, people to
fight for and lives to protect. However, he can
never get his ending because he was never a
part of the story to begin with. It was nothing
more than a fleeting dream, a mere illusion of
happiness that he could never attain. Perhaps
it had already been over before it even began.
Indulging in something that never belonged.
